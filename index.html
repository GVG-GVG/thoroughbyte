THE PROBLEM

Raw time tells you almost nothing.

Every buyer at the under-tack show sees the same clock. But raw time is distorted by wind, track condition, set position, and distance. Two horses can post identical times and have completely different athletic profiles.

ThoroughByte goes beyond the clock. We decompose each breeze into its biomechanical components, normalize for environmental variables, and produce a single score that actually predicts on-track performance.

HOW IT WORKS

Multi-factor analysis.
One clear answer.

01

Ingest

We capture biomechanical and performance data from every under-tack breeze — time, stride length, gallop-out splits, and deceleration patterns.

02

Normalize

Proprietary adjustments strip out environmental noise — wind, track surface, set position — isolating the horse's true athletic signal.

03

Score

A weighted composite model produces a 0–100 rating and tier classification, calibrated separately for sex and breeze distance.

04

Deliver

You receive ranked reports before sale day — complete with tier ratings, sire analytics, and dam black-type annotations.
